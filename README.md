# Course-App
Our interactive learning platform bridges the gap between expertise and curiosity. Users can access curated video courses offline, stay motivated with personalized progress tracking, and engage in vibrant community forums. With built-in quizzes to certify mastery, our app empowers learners to acquire and refine new skills anytime, anywhere.

# Our interactive learning platform bridges the gap between expertise and curiosity. Features include:

* Curated Video Courses with offline access.
* Progress Tracking & personalized milestones.
* Community Forums for peer-to-peer support.
* Quizzes to certify skill mastery.

Empowering learners to master new skills anytime, anywhere.
